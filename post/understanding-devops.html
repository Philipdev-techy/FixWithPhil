Understanding DevOps: A Beginner’s Guide

Posted on July 17, 2025

DevOps is the practice of combining development (Dev) and IT operations (Ops). It focuses on:

  • Automation of processes
  • Continuous integration and delivery (CI/CD)
  • Improved collaboration
  • Faster deployment and recovery

If you're learning Linux, Git, and cloud, you're already on the DevOps path!
